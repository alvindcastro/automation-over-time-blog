description: "How automation moved from an individual effort to a collective capability."
pubDate: 2025-12-05
phase: 6

---

At some point, ownership shifted.

Automation stopped being described as something built by one group and started being referenced as a tool available to multiple teams.

This didn’t happen through formal announcements. It happened through shared language and repeated use.

Clear boundaries made this possible. Responsibility was defined. Expectations were documented. No one assumed automation would handle everything.

That clarity kept trust intact.

Automation didn’t become more powerful by being shared.

It became more valuable by being understood.
